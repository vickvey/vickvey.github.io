About & Research Interests

I am a final-year Computer Science student at IISER Bhopal, working at the intersection of data science, machine learning, and applied optimization. My work focuses on developing data-driven models to understand and analyze complex systems, particularly in settings involving real-world data and uncertainty.

As a researcher, I am interested in building end-to-end computational pipelines — from data acquisition and preprocessing to modeling, evaluation, and interpretation. I have experience working with large-scale datasets, time-series data, and network-based systems, with an emphasis on extracting meaningful insights and supporting decision-making.

I am particularly interested in applying machine learning and optimization techniques to domains such as environmental monitoring, sensing systems, and large-scale data-driven decision platforms, where computational methods can have real-world impact.
